roommateHUB

2021

Role

Entrepreneur, Designer & Coder

Team of 6 people


Problem

The main problem that sparked the creation of our app was the lack of such apps on the market. All the organisational, co-living apps out there did not satisfy the need of our target group: one sharable online space for roommates to organise their apartments and the chores within it.


Solution

We had a white canvas. Our goal was also the starting point: the app had to be enough so that our target group wouldn’t feel the need for any other home-sharing app. An all in one space that we created with syncable and adaptable features.

Bollygge

2021

Role

Designer & Coder

Team of 6 people


Problem

As a team, our main problem was designing a website from a particular dance school’s perspective. We had to look through the eyes of a business that had to sell their product but was at the same time showing the art behind it. Research, research, research.


Solution

After researching the dance itself, we had to make choices that would allow for the website to attract our target group to a point where they would feel intrigued and interested to participate. We achieved that with the creation of personas, scenarios, affinity diagrams and usability testing.
